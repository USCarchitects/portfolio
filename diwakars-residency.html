Diwakar's Residency

Welcome To Diwaker Residency, A Testament To Contemporary Architectural Excellence, Crafted By Urban Structures Architecture. Nestled In The Serene Locale Of BCMC Layout, Kanakapura Road, Bengaluru, Karnataka, This Project Redefines Urban Living With A Blend Of Modern Aesthetics And Functional Design.

Location Of Project:

Bangalore

Year Of Construction:

2021

Category Of Project:

Trun Key Project

Key Features:

Modern Design

 Diwaker Residency Showcases Contemporary Design Elements, With Clean Lines, Elegant Finishes, And High-Quality Materials. The Architecture Seamlessly Blends With The Surrounding Environment, Creating A Harmonious Living Space

Spacious Layouts

The Project Offers A Range Of Unit Sizes To Cater To Different Needs. From Cozy Apartments To Expansive Homes, Each Unit Is Designed To Provide Ample Space For Comfortable Living.

Amenities

Residents Can Enjoy A Host Of Amenities Including A Well-Equipped Gym, Landscaped Gardens, Children's Play Area, And A Community Hall. These Facilities Are Designed To Enhance The Quality Of Life And Foster A Sense Of Community.

Sustainable Living

Diwaker Residency Is Committed To Sustainable Living. The Project Incorporates Eco-Friendly Practices Such As Rainwater Harvesting, Energy-Efficient Lighting, And Waste Management Systems, Ensuring A Minimal Environmental Footprint.

Security

The Safety And Security Of Residents Are Paramount. The Project Features 24/7 Security Services, CCTV Surveillance, And Secure Access Points To Ensure Peace Of Mind For All Residents.
