Project Details

Pott House

In Collaboration With A Former Architecture Classmate, This Residential Projectembraced Design Flexibility. The Key Challenge Involved The Loose Blackcotton Soil, Rendering Traditional Foundations Impractical. To Address This,Pile Foundations Were Implemented, Transferring Weight To Deeper, Stable Soil Layers. This Reduced Overall Building Load Optimizing Both Structural Efficiency and Project Cost.

Location Of Project:

Hyderabad

Year Of Construction:

2021

Category Of Project:

Residential

Key Features:

Wood as Primary
