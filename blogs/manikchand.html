Scope of the Project:

Our project aimed to create a contemporary and functional living space by focusing on:

  • Living and Dining Areas
  • Kitchen
  • Bedrooms
  • Balconies and bathrooms

Key Features:

Modernizing Interio:
‍
At House of Hiranandani, we believe in creating spaces that reflect contemporary aesthetics while maximizing functionality. Our renovation process includes:

  • Upgrading Flooring: We replaced outdated flooring with high-quality, durable materials that enhance the overall aesthetic appeal and comfort.
  • Wall Finishes: Using modern paint techniques and textures, we transformed dull walls into vibrant focal points.
  • Cabinetry and Storage: Custom-built cabinets and smart storage solutions were installed to optimize space and declutter living areas.

Optimizing Space:
‍Effective space utilization is key to comfortable living. Our approach involves:

  • Reconfiguring Layouts: We redesigned floor plans to improve flow and functionality, ensuring each area serves its purpose efficiently.
  • Multifunctional Furniture: Introducing furniture that serves multiple purposes, such as sofa beds and expandable dining tables, to adapt to various needs.
