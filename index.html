Pott House

This Residential project embraced design flexibility. The key challenge involved the loose blackcotton soil, rendering traditional foundations impractical. To address this,pile foundations were implemented, transferring weight to deeper, stable soillayers. This reduced overall building load optimizing both structural efficiencyand project cost.
